Crispy Chicken Burger

Price: $6.99

Sink your teeth into our Crispy Chicken Burger! Featuring a perfectly seasoned, golden-fried chicken fillet, topped with fresh lettuce, juicy tomato slices, and creamy mayo, all nestled in a soft, toasted brioche bun. It's the perfect balance of crunch and flavor!

Classic Beef Burger

Price: $7.49

Our Classic Beef Burger is a timeless favorite! Juicy, flame-grilled beef patty layered with melted cheddar cheese, crisp lettuce, vine-ripened tomatoes, crunchy pickles, and a dollop of our signature burger sauce. All served on a freshly toasted sesame seed bun. Every bite is a classic delight!

Golden French Fries

Price: $2.99 (Small) | $3.99 (Large)

Crisp on the outside, fluffy on the inside—our Golden French Fries are the ultimate sidekick to any meal! Made from farm-fresh potatoes, fried to perfection, and lightly salted for that irresistible flavor.

Crispy Onion Rings

Price: $3.49 (Small) | $4.49 (Large)

Take your taste buds on a crunchy adventure with our Crispy Onion Rings. Sweet onions dipped in our signature seasoned batter and fried until golden brown. Perfect for dipping or enjoying on their own!

Oreo Milkshake

Price: $4.99

Indulge in creamy bliss with our Oreo Milkshake! Made with rich vanilla ice cream, blended with crushed Oreo cookies, and topped with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate syrup. Every sip is a cookies-and-cream dream!

Coca-Cola
